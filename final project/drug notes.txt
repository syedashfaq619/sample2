1.Traxemice acid(Tablet)
This medication is used to treat heavy bleeding during your menstrual period. 

2.Lupron(Injection)
Lupron is used in men to treat the symptoms of prostate cancer. Leuprolide only treats the symptoms of prostate cancer and does not treat the cancer itself.
Lupron Depot is used in women to treat symptoms of endometriosis (overgrowth of uterine lining outside of the uterus) or uterine fibroids.

3.Uliprstal(Tablet)
Ulipristal is used to prevent pregnancy.

4.Leuprolide
Leuprolide is used to treat advanced prostate cancer. It is also used to treat uterine fibroids and endometriosis.
